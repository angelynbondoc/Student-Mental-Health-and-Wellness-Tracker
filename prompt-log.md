# Prompt Log
## Role: KM Analyst
## Project: Student Mental Health & Wellness Tracker

## Entry 1
**Prompt:** What KM framework best suits a mental health app?
**Response:** SECI Model was recommended due to tacit-explicit 
knowledge transformation cycle.
**Reflection:** Helped clarify why SECI fits better than CoP.

## Entry 2
**Prompt:** How do I map SECI components to app features?
**Response:** Each SECI phase maps to a specific feature 
(journal, sharing, library, tracker).
**Reflection:** Made the architecture document much clearer.

## Entry 3
**Prompt:** How do I write a problem statement for a KM report?
**Response:** Focus on the knowledge gap between feeling and 
understanding mental health.
**Reflection:** Strengthened the conceptual direction of the app.

## Entry 4
**Prompt:** What is the difference between tacit and explicit 
knowledge in mental health context?
**Response:** Tacit = personal feelings/emotions. 
Explicit = documented advice and strategies.
**Reflection:** This distinction became the core of our KM approach.

## Entry 5
**Prompt:** How can a mental health app encourage knowledge 
internalization?
**Response:** Through habit trackers, daily exercises, 
and self-growth insights.
**Reflection:** Directly influenced the app feature concepts.
