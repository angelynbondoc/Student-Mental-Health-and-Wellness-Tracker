# PM Reflection

## What decisions were hardest
One of the hardest decisions I faced was figuring out the overall flow of the system. My groupmates would often come to me with questions about certain adjustments, and I had to think carefully before saying yes or no, because one wrong call could affect how the whole system works. Deciding which features to keep or remove was also difficult. Even though they gave me the freedom to make those calls, I still had to make sure the system would work properly and would be easy for a regular person to use, not just someone who already understands it.

## How you handled a conflict or blocker,
In terms of conflict, the biggest challenge was the lack of communication early on. My groupmates would message me one by one with separate questions, and at some point it got overwhelming. I noticed it was slowing things down because I was the only one passing information around. I eventually told the group that while I was happy to help coordinate, they also needed to talk to each other directly. Over time, that shift helped. The conversations became more open and the progress picked up.

## What you would do differently.
If I could do things differently, I would set clear communication rules from the very start, like a group channel where everyone posts updates, instead of letting the habit of going through me alone develop. I would also document decisions early on so that every adjustment to the flow or features is written down somewhere the whole team can see. That way, if someone questions a decision later, there is a clear record of why it was made.
